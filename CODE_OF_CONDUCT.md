# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming research community, we as
contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in QIL a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and giving constructive, evidence-based
  scientific critique.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism and focusing on what is best for
  the community and the correctness of the research.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
personal or political attacks, and publishing others' private information
without consent.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces
when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported to the project maintainer at `1912574a@umich.mx`. All complaints will
be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
